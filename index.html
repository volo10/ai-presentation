UNDERSTANDING LLMs

Probabilistic Models & Context Windows

LLMs Predict Probabilities

LLMs predict the most probable next token based on your input.

"Capital of France is" ‚Üí P("Paris") = 95%

üéØ Better Prompt = Higher Probability

Specific prompts narrow the probability distribution toward your desired output

The Context Window

LLMs have a limited memory (context window) - typically 8K-200K tokens.

When context fills up, older information may be summarized or lost - this is why starting fresh chats for new tasks is important!
